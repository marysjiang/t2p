t2p

This project is based off of Janet Murray’s abstraction framework for creating variation in interactive narratives, proposed in “A Tale of Two Boyfriends: A Literary Abstraction Strategy for Creating Meaningful Character Variation.” The project departs from previous iterations examining heterosexual love triangles. It instead focuses on lesbian love triangles between a woman in the middle (GWEN), the Girlfriend of Obligation (G.O.), and the Girlfriend of Desire (G.D.). Tale of Two Girlfriends is a tool that allows authors to explore variants in stories and generate dramatic contrast in parameterized, five-act storylines based off of their choices.
